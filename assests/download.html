PRODUCT SUPPORT

FAQS

Does Mivi Roam 2 supports my Phone model?

Yes, it can connect to all the smartphones (Android and iOS) and devices which have Bluetooth connectivity.

Can I connect Mivi Roam 2 to my Laptop?

Yes, the speaker can be connected to all laptops which have Bluetooth connectivity.

How do I ensure the best possible Bluetooth signal for my Roam 2 speaker?
  • - Make sure the distance between your speaker and the paired device (such as the smartphone, tablet, computer) should not be more than 10 meters.
  • - Make sure there is a direct line of sight between your speaker and the paired device. Ensure that there are no structures hindering the signals, such as walls and doors, between the speaker and paired devices.
Is Mivi Roam 2 waterproof?

It comes with IPX7 rating which makes it splash-resistant, so you need not worry about little splashes of water.

What is the playtime of Roam 2 speaker after a single charge?

Once you full charge Roam 2, you can enjoy up to 24 hours of non-stop music at 70% volume.

Does dust build-up affects the performance of the speaker?

No, the premium and compact design of Roam 2 keeps dust at bay and your speaker’s performance unaffected.
